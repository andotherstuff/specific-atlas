        The thing as a whole

        Donald Judd refused illusion. A work was not a picture of something — it was a specific object, a real thing in real space.

        This atlas treats his life the same way: people, works, places, and ideas as discrete objects, each one a signed event living on the Nostr network. Pull a node to read it; follow its edges to travel time, geography, and thought.
