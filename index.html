          The thing as a whole

          Donald Judd refused illusion. A work was not a picture of something—it was a specific object, a real thing in real space.

          This atlas treats his life the same way: people, works, places, and ideas as discrete objects, each one a signed record you can trace to whoever added it. Pull a node to read it; follow its edges to travel time, geography, and thought.
